Construction Kenya is a digital publication for the construction community.

Mission Statement
To inform and empower people with factual, intelligent, and compelling content that provides solutions to problems – while, ultimately, driving profitable customer actions.
Our mission compels us to focus on exclusive research-driven stories, which allows us to step away from the highly tempting thrill of chasing the tail of ‘breaking news’.
Code of Ethics
We abide by the editorial values of honesty, credibility, fairness, and independence – giving no priority to commercial or political interests over professional duty.
Advertising Solutions
In addition to presenting information that improves people’s lives, we also connect businesses with their customers through display ads, brand partnerships, etc.
